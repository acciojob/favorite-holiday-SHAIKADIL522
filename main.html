Manali


Manali is a beautiful place to visit in the winter. It is full of mountains covered with snow and offers stunning views of nature all around.

Top 5 Attraction Spots

  1. Rohtang Pass — A breathtaking mountain pass at 3,978 meters above sea level.
  2. Solang Valley — Known for its stunning snowy landscapes and adventure activities.
  3. Hadimba Temple — An ancient wooden temple nestled among tall deodar trees.
  4. Kullu Mandir Devta Raghunath — A historically significant temple in the heart of Kullu.
  5. Beas River — A serene and scenic river perfect for rafting and relaxation.

Must-Try Foods in Manali

  • Siddu — A traditional wheat flour dumpling filled with poppy seeds and nuts.
  • Chha Gosht — A rich and flavorful lamb curry cooked in local spices.
  • Trout Fish — Freshly caught fish from the icy rivers, grilled to perfection.
  • Pahari Daal — A hearty and warming lentil dish unique to the hills.
  • Patande — A sweet local pancake made from wheat and served with honey.
